Beneficial Ownership Registration

What is Beneficial Ownership?

Beneficial Ownership (BO) refers to the natural person(s) who ultimately own or control a company, even if they aren't the registered shareholders. In South Africa, CIPC regulations require companies to disclose this information to prevent financial crimes like money laundering and to ensure complete transparency.

At Laizer Consultation, we understand these regulations can be complex and time-consuming. We simplify this process, providing a seamless and professional service to ensure your Beneficial Ownership status is fully compliant with CIPC requirements. With us, you get the peace of mind that your business is operating with integrity and transparency, protecting you from non-compliance penalties.

Get Compliant for R280

Requirements:

  • ID: A clear copy of the ID or passport of the beneficial owner(s).
  • Cor14.3 Company Certificate: A copy of your company's official registration certificate.
  • Email & Phone Number: The valid contact details for the beneficial owner.

CSD Registration

What is CSD Registration?

Registering on the Central Supplier Database (CSD) is a vital step for any business looking to work with the South African government. This single, unified database serves as the official source of all supplier information for government departments. We simplify the entire process, ensuring your business is correctly registered with all necessary documentation, including tax and company details. By using our service, you gain a verified CSD number and reference, which is essential for tendering on government contracts and establishing your credibility as a reliable supplier.

Get Registered for R250

Requirements:

  • Cellphone Number for OTP
  • ID or Company Registration Number
  • Bank Account Information
  • Directors/Members Information

COIDA Registration

What is COIDA Registration?

Registration with the Compensation Fund for Occupational Injuries and Diseases (COIDA) is mandatory for all employers. It provides insurance coverage for employees who are injured on duty or contract occupational diseases. We handle the entire registration process, ensuring you receive your Letter of Good Standing and remain compliant.

Get Compliant for R3000

Requirements:

  • Cor14.3 (Company Certificate)
  • ID Copy of Director(s)
  • Bank Confirmation Letter

Company Re-instatement

What is Company Re-instatement?

If your company has been de-registered by the CIPC for non-compliance (usually due to failing to file annual returns), it can be re-instated. This process restores the company to the official register, allowing it to trade legally again. We handle the application and all necessary paperwork to get your business back in good standing.

Reinstate for R750

Requirements:

  • Cor14.3 Doc (Company Certificate)

Annual Returns

What are Annual Returns?

Every company registered with the CIPC is required to file an annual return to confirm that it is still active and to update its information. Failing to do so can lead to penalties and eventual de-registration. We ensure your annual returns are filed correctly and on time, keeping your company compliant and avoiding any legal issues.

File for R250

Requirements:

  • Company Registration Number
